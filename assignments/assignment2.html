This was a fleece onesie that I sewed up when my cat Tiger kept popping new mast cell tumors on his skin as fast as the vet could take them off… so we agreed to shave him and see how many more were starting. There were hundreds. The good news is that the cancer was treatable with very inexpensive meds ($8/3 month supply) and the meds kept him well for quite a few more years. And we stopped trying to remove all the little tumors… and he wore his Tiger Suit (with dignity! unlike poor Shaman) until his fur grew back, which only took a couple months.
